5 AI LEVERAGE MOVES

  • 1 AI Client Onboarding Automation

    Set up an AI assistant that welcomes new clients, answers FAQs, collects onboarding info, and preps them for success — without needing manual back-and-forth emails.

  • 2 Automated Session Summaries

    Use AI to generate and send session notes to your clients after coaching or service sessions, reinforcing key insights and next steps instantly.

  • 3 Personalized Drip Support

    Create a simple AI-driven sequence that checks in with clients after milestones (e.g., "Week 2 Progress Check-In"), making your support feel personal without draining your time.

  • 4 Proposal or Quote Automation

    Let AI draft customized proposals, offers, or renewal packages based on a few client inputs — helping you close deals faster.

  • 5 Internal Knowledge Base for Your Team

    Build a basic AI knowledge base trained on your SOPs, FAQs, and client processes — so your (future) team can self-serve answers without pulling you into every minor question.
